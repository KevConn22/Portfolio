Amphibious Rover Design Concept

The general concept of this particular design is to create an all-surface rover for the exploration of deep space. Naturally, there are obstacles when we work with foreign surfaces: namely, we do not know what the surface is comprised of. With that being the case, the need arises to have a multi-functionality rover that can withstand different surface mediums, including both solid and liquid surfaces. This rover has the capability to be on both, boasting:

  • Extendable legs with stress-limiting design
  • Retractable bay doors to contain the body and legs during subnautical situations
  • Variable pressure system to alter buoyancy, allowing submersion in aqueous environments
While currently still in the drafting stage, the main timeline is as follows:
  1. Aug. 2023: Finish drafting concept
  2. Dec. 2023: Finish SolidWorks model/drawing
  3. May 2023: Finish manufacturing hardware components
  4. July 2023: Complete assembly and begin testing
